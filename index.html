The Lore

— how woolbound came to be —

✦

Woolbound began as a small spell — a school project, a box of wool, and two hands learning to create something from nothing.

Every piece is finger-knitted by hand, one loop at a time, with no machines and no shortcuts. Each pouch, each scarf, each cosy thing carries the time and intention of its maker.

We believe handmade things carry a kind of magic that factory-made things never can. When you hold something Woolbound, you hold something real.
